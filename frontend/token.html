Token Exchange

Earn tokens by restoring rainwater and use them for rewards later.

How It Works

Every time you collect and restore 100 liters of rainwater, you earn 1 token.

You can accumulate tokens and later redeem them for useful rewards or services.

To get tokens, you must upload a photo as proof of your rainwater restoration efforts.

Exchange Your Rainwater for Tokens

Important Notes

  • Minimum 100 liters required to exchange for 1 token.
  • Ensure the photo clearly shows your rainwater harvesting setup.
  • Tokens will be credited after verification.
  • You can use tokens later for special discounts and services.
