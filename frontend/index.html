What is Rainwater Harvesting?

Rainwater harvesting is the collection and storage of rainwater for reuse before it reaches the ground. It helps in conserving water and preventing urban flooding.

Benefits

  • Reduces dependence on groundwater
  • Prevents soil erosion
  • Cost-effective and sustainable
  • Promotes environmental awareness
